Welcome to Our Space!

Cosmic Coffee was founded in 2021. Though we may be new to your neighborhood but we are not alien to the world of coffee. In fact, we have been brewing coffee for nearly a decade in Los Angeles, CA. Here at Cosmic Coffee, we value our community, inclusivity, and seek to provide a friendly atmoshpere where you can chill, chat, and enjoy an out of this world cup of coffee!
